Products

Automatic stand system

This system automatically detects the scenario in which the position of Stand should be (Retracted or Applied) and executes it immediately by use of our patented technology Thereby ensuring riders safety and comfort.

Vehicle Horn Control Assembly

VHCA is a state-of-the-art system which helps in entirely regulating noise pollution caused due to the horns of vehicles. In that particular vicinity where our system in inculcated the vehicle owners can automatically abide to the honking regulations applied in that area.
